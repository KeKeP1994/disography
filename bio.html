Sleeping With Sirens

Sleeping with Sirens is an American post-hardcore band from Orlando, Florida currently residing in Grand Rapids, Michigan. The band was formed in 2009 by members of For All We Know, Broadway, and Paddock Park. They are currently signed to Rise Records and have released three full-length albums and an acoustic EP. The group is known primarily for the versatility of vocalist Kellin Quinn's tenor leggiero vocal range.

Kellin Quinn

Kellin Quinn Bostwick, singer/songwriter, 26, married to Katelynn and the proud father to Copeland, is well known as the lead singer of Sleeping with Sirens. Sleeping with Sirens currently has 3 albums out (With Ears To See and Eyes to Hear – 2010, Let’s Cheers To This – 2011, and If You Were A Movie, This Would Be Your Soundtrack – 2012) Their new album now release date is June 4th, 2013. Kellin hasn’t always been in Sleeping with Sirens – previously he was in Closer 2 Closure, For All We Know, Screenplay, and Our Name In City Light. Not to mention all of the single he’s been featured in with other amazing bands such as Pierce the Veil, Memphis May Fire, The Words We Use, and The Last Word to name a few.

Jack Fowler

Jack Fowler (born 25 January 1991) is an American rock musician. He is currently the guitarist for Sleeping with Sirens and was previously active in the band Broadway, where he also played guitar. Fowler was active before joining Sleeping with Sirens as a guitarist in Broadway. With the group, he released the album Kingdoms in which he could be heard as a guitarist. On 19 May 2011 it was announced that he left Broadway to play with Sleeping with Sirens. He was a big part in making the four albums that Sleeping With Sirens has produced, (With Eyes To Hear And Ears To See), (Let’s Cheer To This), (If You Were A Movie This Would Be Your Soundtrack) and (Feel.)

Gabe Barham

Gabriel "Gabe" Barham (born October 31, 1989, in Muskegon, Michigan). He is the current drummer for post-hardcore bands For All We Know and Sleeping with Sirens. He has since moved to Orlando, Florida to perform with Sleeping with Sirens. Barham was the original drummer for the Christian hardcore band For All We Know, and is still in the line up. Barham was the second drummer of Post Hardcore band Sleeping with Sirens, replacing Alex Kaladjian after his short lived position in the band. Barham was featured in both Sleeping with Sirens demo, and in their full-length debut album, With Ears To See and Eyes to Hear.

Justin Hills

Justin Hills is an American bassist, who currently performs with Christian hardcore band For All We Know and Post Hardcore band Sleeping with Sirens. Hills is the current bassist of Christian hardcore band For All We Know. He has been featured on their debut EP; we're about to Change. At the same time as he performs with For All We Know, Hills is also in Sleeping with Sirens, as the bassist and backing vocalist. He was featured on their demo album and their debut Rise Records release, With Ears to See and Eyes to Hear.
